Web Site Critiques

Question List

  1. What was your first impression of this site?
  2. How does this site organize the flow of information?
  3. Does the site structure make sense or does it confuse you? Why?
  4. Does the site graphics/layout/multimedia enhance the site or detract from it? Why?
  5. How long did it take for the home page to load? Was the load time reasonable?
  6. Was the text color and size readable? If the background color or image was used, did it enhance or detract from the site?
  7. Did the site have a navigation bar or other navigation tool? Was teh navigation well done?
  8. Did the site have a color scheme or look and feel that was repeated on all the pages?
  9. How does the site respond to smaller window sizes (responsive design)?
  10. What does this site offer that was unusual or very well done?

endoftheworld2012

Visit endoftheworld2012

  1. My first impression of this site was confusion. I didn't and still dont know exactly what this site is used for and it feels like unless this site was pitched to you for a specific reason, no one would know what to do when they get on this site.
  2. In my opinion, very poorly. There are 3 places to click and all 3 take you to different websites for very very specific things.
  3. It confuses me. The name of the website and what is actually on the website seem to be very unrelated. There is a button for how to access the actual website but you have to go through a Domain Backorder which I will not be doing.
  4. For what I can see, the graphics are clean and appealing to the eyes.
  5. The load time for me was very quick. I didn't feel the need to exit the page and reload it.
  6. I think the text size and color make it very readable. The background color, in my opinion, enhances the page simply because I like the color and it doesnt feel like a flashbang when the page is opened.
  7. No navigation bar, only buttons to what is called 'Related Searches' and then the link to the Domain Backorder.
  8. The background of the page did not change unless you clicked on the backorder button.
  9. The site adjusts accordingly to smaller window sizes. The background and front text stay relative in size and margins even when the window gets smaller.
  10. To me it was how fast all the links load. Regardless of what you click on it is very fast and takes you to the next page and load that page very quick.
